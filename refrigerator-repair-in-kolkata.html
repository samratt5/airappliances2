Common refrigerator problems in Kolkata — causes and solutions

Kolkata’s humid, coastal climate often leads to condensation, water leakage, and faster wear on door gaskets and drain systems. Power fluctuations can also impact inverter compressor and PCB life. Here’s how we address the most frequent fridge issues.

Not cooling

Cause: Refrigerant leak, faulty compressor, blocked airflow.

Fix: Leak test, charge, relay/capacitor check, fan replacement, coil cleaning.

Over-freezing

Cause: Faulty thermostat or sensor, stuck damper.

Fix: Sensor calibration, damper repair, thermostat replacement.

Water leakage

Cause: Clogged drain, cracked drain pan, condensation.

Fix: Drain unclogging, tray replacement, gasket alignment.

Noisy operation

Cause: Worn fan bearings, compressor hum, loose panels.

Fix: Fan motor, leveling, vibration dampers, compressor check.

Ice maker not working

Cause: Inlet valve, sensor, temperature imbalance.

Fix: Valve replacement, defrost cycle audit, PCB check.

Bad smell

Cause: Drain stagnation, mold, long power cuts.

Fix: Deep clean, drain flush, gasket sanitization.

High power usage

Cause: Dirty coils, failing door seals, aging compressor.

Fix: Coil cleaning, gasket replacement, inverter diagnostic.

Control issues

Cause: PCB/inverter faults due to voltage spikes.

Fix: Board repair/replacement, surge protection advice.

For DIY maintenance tips, see Family Handyman, Bob Vila, and Consumer Reports. Always disconnect power before inspecting components.
